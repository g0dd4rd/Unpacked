19 - Geometry in music

In this chapter, we will discover that music and its elements have shapes and thus contains geometry.

Major triad

The major triad forms a triangle shape in any key, in this case it's E major triad / chord.

Major seventh arpeggio

The major seventh arpeggio forms a trapezoid in any key, in this cas it's E major seventh arpeggio / chord.

Dominant seventh arpeggio

The dominant seventh arpeggio / chord forms a trapezoid shape in any key, in this case it's E dominant seventh arpeggio / chord.

Minor triad

The minor triad forms a isosceles triangle shape in any key, in this case it's E minor triad / chord.

Minor seventh arpeggio

The minor seventh arpeggio forms a isosceles trapezoid in any key, in this cas it's E minor seventh arpeggio / chord.

Diminished triad

The diminished triad forms a equilateral triangle shape in any key, in this case it's E diminished triad / chord.

Half-diminished seventh arpeggio

The half-diminished arpeggio forms almost a diamond shape in any key, in this case it's E half-diminished triad / chord.

Diminished seventh arpeggio

The diminished arpeggio forms a diamond shape in any key, in this case it's E diminished triad / chord.

Augmented triad

The augmented triad forms a equilateral triangle shape in any key, in this case it's E augmented triad / chord.

Augmented seventh arpeggio

The augmented seventh arpeggio forms a irregular trapezoid shape in any key, in this case it's E augmented arepggio / chord.
